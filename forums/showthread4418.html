Posted: Dec 7th, 2020 05:05 #185 “Quote” Edit Post
This site was so well-organized, I can't help but feel it was the main reason why the game had the following it did. It had everything anyone at any level could ask for: region specific community sections, tournament announcements, strategy sections by game/character, frame data for all characters, a casual section... and the mod team did a fantastic job of curating data and keeping conversations on-topic; so much so that one could go back to those threads even now and still find incredibly useful information on whatever game/character they were looking for. Very few sites can make such a boast.

I, and many people, owe a lot to this site, and a big thank you goes to Castel and everyone involved in maintaining it. To this day, I still occasionally bring this site up with my friends and tell stories of the people I met, and the experiences I had because of it. I also can't ignore that these experiences had some influence on me in the path I took in my career in the game industry.

That said, the site may be gone, but the memories shared will last, and we will continue to talk about them. Perhaps like my tagline says, it's simply us "livin' in the past," but they are memories worth having.

Thank you.
