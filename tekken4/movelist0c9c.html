Steve Fox Move List

Notes Whenever you're into a stance with Steve using (3_4_3+4) you can keep holding the button or buttons and change stances immediately by tapping joystick directions. Up or down will go into Left Weave or Right Weave depending on what side of the screen you are. Forward will go into Ducking and tapping back will make Steve Sway.
