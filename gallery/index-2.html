Official Namco Art

Tekken Tag Tournament 2 Character CG
TekkenTag Tournament 2 character CG renders saved in PNG format on transparent background for easy use in making your Tekken fan art.
Tekken: Blood Vengeance
High definition screens of Tekken: Blood Vengeance action CG movie produced by Namco Bandai Games
Tekken 6:BR Character CG
Tekken 6:BR character CG renders saved in PNG format on transparent background for easy use in making your Tekken fan art.
Tekken 6 Character CG
Tekken 6 character CG renders saved in PNG format on transparent background for easy use in making your Tekken fan art.
Tekken 5:DR Character CG
Tekken 5 Dark Resurrection character CG renders on white backgrounds for easy extracting. Time release characters will be added as we get them.
Tekken 5 Intro CG
High resolution screen renders of Tekken 5's arcade intro movie. Prime examples of Namco's award winning CG wizardry.
Tekken 5 Costume Designs
Costumes designs by various Japanese artist used to create the PS2 exclusive extra outfits.
Tekken 5 Character CG
Tekken 5 character CG renders on white backgrounds for easy extracting. Aside from a few alternate renders, all characters are represented.
Nina: Death By Degrees
Some official Namco artwork for the spinoff Tekken action game Nina: Death By Degrees. Hot stuff!
Tekken 4 Sketches
Some original Namco Tekken 4 character development sketches, showing character outfit designs, special move animations and ending story boards.
Tekken 4 Character CG
All default Tekken 4 characters' full body CG renders on white backgrounds for easy extracting. Time release characters will be added if we ever get them.
Tekken 4 Concept Art
All original Namco Tekken 4 characters concept art drawings. Both outfits for all characters including time release and a bonus Hwoarang drawing.
Tekken Promotional Art
Various original Namco art works used for promotional tools such as posters, post cards, magazine covers and collectable cards.
Namco 2000 Collector Cards
Unfortunately these cool cards were only released in Japan. But we tracked all 81 of them all down and ran them through the scanner, front and back!
Tekken 3 Concept Art
We finally got our hands on some large renditions of Tekken 3 character concept art and we wouldn't dream of keeping these amazing drawings from you.
Tekken 2 Retro Series
We had never ever seen these images on the web anywhere until we found some old Tekken 2 Guides in a small import store. Extremely rare stuff here folks!
Tekken 3 CG Intro Captures
Twelve high resolution screen renders of Tekken 3's PlayStation intro movie. Prime examples of Namco's award winning CG wizardry.
Reiko Nagase 2000 Calendar
This calendar was included with Tekken Tag at some Japanese stores. The Ridge Racer hottie has been our favorite video game babe for years. We couldn't resist!
1999 Edition Trading Cards
Only 9 Tekken related cards to be found in the entire 1999 collection. Some cool conceptual art work can be seen on the back of the cards.
Symphony Orchestra Calendar
This gallery features 12 excellent scans of the rare Tekken 3 calendar released by Namco in 1998. You won't see the Tekken fighters in this classy setting very often.
