Tackles & Tackle Attacks

All characters have to ability to tackle the opponent during a run, check the running system section for more details on how to tackle during a run. A few characters can perform a Tackle from standing position.
Character Command
Craig Marduk VTS 1+2
Devil Jin FC+1+2 or d+1+2 or d/b+1+2
Kazuya Mishima FC+1+2 or d+1+2 or d/b+1+2
King FC+1+2
Paul Phoenix FC+1+2 or d/b+1+2

Tackle Escapes

All characters can escape tackles and most of the tackle attacks. A few exceptions to the rule include Craig's Spear Tackle and Asuka's Ultimate Tackle.
Character Place Move Escape Command Note
All Characters Front Tackle 2 (Avoid Tackle) #1
    Tackle 1+2 (Tackle Over Turn) #2
  Front Ultimate Tackle (Asuka) 1+2 (Tackle Over Turn) #4
  Front Spear Tackle (Craig) 1+2 (Avoid Tackle) #5
  Back Tackle None  
  Front 1st Mounted Punch (Left) 2  
    1st Mounted Punch (Right) 1  
    4th Mounted Punch (Left) 2  
    4th Mounted Punch (Right) 1  
    2nd - 3rd - 5th Mounted Punch None  
  Back 1st Mounted Punch (Left) 1  
    1st Mounted Punch (Right) 2  
    2nd to 5th Mounted Punch None  
  Front Cross Arm Lock 1+2,2,2,2,2  
    Leg Cross Lock 1+2,1,1,1,1  
    Arm Twist None  
    Ultimate S.T.F. (King) None  
    Ultimate Punishment (Paul) None #3
Foot Notes #1 Input within 2 frames for a running tackle and within 4 frames for a standing tackle after the hit frame.
#2 Input right before your back hits the ground. After over turning the tackle you can use tackle attacks.
#3 The 1st right punch is escapable, the attacks after that are not escapable.
#4 Asuka's Ultimate Tackle cannot be avoided, only overturned after hitting the ground.
#5 A long range Spear Tackle cannot be avoided. See Craig's movelist for more Spear Tackle details.
