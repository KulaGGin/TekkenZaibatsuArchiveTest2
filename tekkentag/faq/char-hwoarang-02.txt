INTRODUCTION
------------
I created this FAQ for people who want some good, damaging
Hwoarang juggles. On this FAQ, I decided to display every
single way in which Hwoarang can setup & juggle an opponent.

(After Hwoarang's stun move(s), opponents can be knocked in 
the air!)  

However, some of you may think that "These juggles are old!". 
On the other hand, some of you may think "I ain't seen that 
one before". So I'd just like to say that all of these juggles 
displayed on my FAQ are juggles that I have found out, and use 
myself. (Well... I only use the really powerful ones! Others are
just here for show)

NOTES ON JUGGLES STARTERS
-------------------------
In TTT, Hwoarang has a total of 11 juggles starters (including
stun starters). These are: Uppercut, Fire Cracker, Sky Rocket, Plasma Blade,
Grand Theft (Plasma Blade), Right Heel Lance (LFF), Left Heel Lance (RFF),
(RFF) Super Stun Kick, Flamingo Flick Kick, (RS) Snap Kick, and
(RS) Straight Kick 

UPPERCUT: (CH) (f,N,d,d/f_d/f)+2   

A fast and sometimes useful juggle starter. Hwoarang has quite alot of 
power to follow up with considering it's quite a weak juggle starter. 
However, a down side is that you can only knock them up with (CH).

FIRE CRACKER: d+4~4

A quite useless juggle starter. This is because, unless you're fighting 
a complete idiot, not a single person will fall of this. However, some 
very powerful juggles lie with this juggle starter.

SKY ROCKET: (f,N,d,d/f_f~d/f)+4

Ha! The daddy of all of Hwoarang's juggle starters. This is because it's 
quite fast, knocks you extremely high which gives you plenty of time to 
think, and can damage an opponent very badly when performing a powerful 
juggle on the way down. This is where all of Hwoarang's juggle power lies.

PLASMA BLADE: (1+2),3

A quite difficult juggle starter to get on people because Hwoarang needs to 
be in 'DISRESPECT'. However, knock them up with this and... well you get the point.

GRAND THEFT (PLASMA BLADE): (3+4),F+4~4  

My favourite juggle starter! No reason really... I just like it.

RIGHT HEEL LANCE (LFF): LFF,b+4  

Powerful juggle starter, strong juggles...nuff said

LEFT HEEL LANCE (RFF): RFF,b+3 

Same as the other one really.

SUPER STUN KICK: (CH) RFF,4

A good powerful stun move. If Hwoarang dashes into an opponet after this, they
can be juggled.

FLAMINGO FLICK KICK: (CH) (3+4),4~f 

A fast right stance kick which Hwoarang posses. On CH, an opponent is knocked into
the air leaving Hwoarang in in trusty RFF. Powerful juggles lie with this move.

FLAMINGO SNAP KICK: RFF,f+4

A useful & fast move. When connected, an opponents head will spring down. Then, as
they begin to fall back, they can be juggled. (normal hit or CH)

RIGHT STANCE STRAIGHT KICK: (CH) (3+4) 4

Useless straight kick with no juggle options what so ever!
