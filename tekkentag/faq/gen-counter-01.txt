X I. I n t r o d u c t i o n X
XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

	In Tekken the best players seem to be unpredictable, 
and theirs nothing more predictable than doing a 10-hit. Thats 
one major reason why 10-hit sucks, another reason is most 10-hits 
can be countered very easily....and not to mention everyone can 
parry the low hits now. Low parrying at the low hits of 10-hits 
seem to be the best way to get out of all 10-hits(only Bryans 
strings have no low hits) becuase you can get some juggles in after
the parry. I thought I'd just put together a few ways to get out of 
all the 10-hits besides attack reversals, and the low parry. This 
part of this faq is mainly for scrubs that have problems dealing 
with 10-hits, and for people that want to punish scrubs that do 
10-hits. I also hope this faq will help get rid of the last few
scrubs that still do 10-hits all the time. In this section, when I
say jab I'm talking about a 1 punch, and when I refer to a fast 
4(right kick)I'm talking about any 4 around 11 frames(like Lei's). 
Take into consideration when you see me write ws+2_4, that not all
characters have the same generic ws+2_4, so it might not work for
certain characters. Please email me if you have anything you would 
like to add in this section, as it not close to being completed.
	Of course the first part of this faq is just a good'ol 
"all the throw escapes in one convienaint place". It's still 
almost impossible to tell which arm your opponent grabs you with 
first, but that seems the only way to be sure of which escape to 
use. For future versions of this faq I'm working on getting the 
timing of the escapes for multithrows. I figured I'd leave out
the names of the throws since alot of people tend to call the
same throws different names.

_____________________
_____Directions______ 


u = tap up          b = tap back
U = hold up         B = hold back
d = tap down        f = tap forward 
D = hold down       F = hold forward
n = neutral         ss = sidestep

____________________
_____Notations______


d/f - tap down+forward
d/b - tap down+back 
u/f - tap up+forward 
u/b - tap up+back                      
D/F - hold down+forward
D/B - hold down+back
U/F - hold up+forward
U/B - hold up+back


WC          - while crouching
RC          - recovers crouching (can be followed by any WS technique)
CH          - counter hit
FC          - full crouch 
JG          - juggles
GB          - guard break
HS          - hunch over stun
WS          - while standing from a crouch
_           - or 
M/-         - mid or inescapable throw
H/-         - high or inescapable throw
~           - do next move extremely fast
QCF         - quarter circle forward      - d,d/f,f
QCB         - quarter circle back         - d,d/b,b
HCF         - half circle forward         - b,d/b,d,d/f,f
HCB         - half circle back            - f,d/f,d,d/b,b

____________________
___Attack buttons___

      (1)  (2)  (5)
             
      (3)  (4)

1 = left punch
2 = right punch
3 = left kick
4 = right kick
5 = tag

____________________
____Attack Levels___

Level:               Defense:

h  - high range      = auto block , B , or duck under
m  - mid  range      = auto block or B
l  - low range       = D/B low block
sm - special mid     = auto block , B , or D/B
T  - throw           = varies
!  - unblockable     = attack reversal
