Attack Reversal Basics

An attack reversal is where you anticipate your opponent's attack and reverse the incoming attack by grabbing the opponents limb and execute a throw or some other form of take down. Not all characters in Tekken Tag Tournament have this ability however.

The following is the frame analysis for each of the character's reversal. We will break it down to frames of execution, frame window where the reversal is active and finally the frame window where your character cannot input a command if you happen to input a reversal at the wrong time.
Character Parry Command Execution Active Window Freeze Window
Jin Kazama (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 8 25
Paul Phoenix (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 8 25
Jun Kazama (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 8 25
Wang Jinrei (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 8 25
Nina Williams (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 8 25
Anna Williams (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 6 28
King (b+1+3_b+2+4) 2 12 22
The damage done by reversals is relative to which move is reversed. Take half the damage points of the move that is being reversed and add 25 points. King can reverse the ankle kick (KND d+3_KND d+4) and the second hit of Hwoarang's RFF f+3,4. Both are low hits but reversable to King non the less.
