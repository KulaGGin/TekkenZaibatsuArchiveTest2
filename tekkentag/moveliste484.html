Foot Notes #1 Going into Flamingo Stance cancels the last hit.

Baek can cancel the Flamingo Stance into some of his regular moves. If you are looking to cancel into a Punch or Right Kick attacks you can just input the proper command. It gets a little trickier if you want to cancel into Left Kick attacks or Double Kick attacks. In order to do this you have to keep holding down Left Kick as you go into Flamingo. For the moves marked with a #2 lets assume you did F+1,2,3 to go into Flamingo and you are now holding Forward and Left Kick.
