Tekken Tag Tournament HD

A remastered Tekken Tag Tournament game in High Definition released exclusively on PlayStation3 along with Tekken Tag Tournament 2 Prologue and the Tekken:Blood Vengeance movie as part of the Tekken Hybrid package.
