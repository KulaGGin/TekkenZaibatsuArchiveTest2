Steve Fox Move List

Notes Steve can do a handful of regular moves while in FLK, these moves are marked with a #1 note. Whenever you go into a stance with Steve using (3_4) you can keep holding the button and change stances fluently by tapping joystick directions. Up or down will go into Left Weave or Right Weave depending on what side of the screen you are, forward will go into Ducking or Long Ducking and tapping back will make Steve Sway.
