Special Item Moves

One of the new features in Tekken 6 is the special item moves. These moves can only be done when a certain item is equipped, in general these moves are pretty slow and do very little to no damage (not all of them actually cause a hit). They are just fun little gimmicks, below is a list of these moves and required item information. If you are lucky enough most of these items can be gained for free in Scenario Campaign Mode.
