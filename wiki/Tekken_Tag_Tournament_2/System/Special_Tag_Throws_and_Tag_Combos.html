This is the list for all the specific team throws and combos available in the game. These moves not only remove recoverable life of your opponent but also safely tag-in your partner. Be sure you know if there is one on your team, as they can always come in handy...

(Thanks to Snakebyte, SSJ-Luffy, Helper, and many others for contributing in the TZ Special Tag Throws Collection Thread. Will be updated as new stuff are discovered)

Note: this is not a list for generic tag throws, for a more detailed list for generic tag throws, go HERE.
