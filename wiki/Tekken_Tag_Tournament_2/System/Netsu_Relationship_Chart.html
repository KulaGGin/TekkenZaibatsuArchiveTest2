Relationship Application


Character relationships (positive, neutral or negative) have an impact on when a character will get enraged from seeing their partner getting hurt in battle.

A desktop application for Windows to mix and match characters in order to see all relevant data is available here .


For general information on the Netsu/Rage system, read the Tagging Primer.
